About

The Mahindra Thar is a rugged off-road SUV known for its iconic design and adventurous spirit. It boasts impressive off-road capabilities with features like high ground clearance and robust chassis. Available with petrol and diesel engine options, it offers a comfortable interior with modern amenities. Its open-top variant provides a unique driving experience. Safety features include airbags, ABS, and parking sensors. The Thar is popular among enthusiasts for its versatility and thrill-seeking potential.
